Privacy Policy

Last updated 2026-05-06

Plain-English summary

  • This site does not require accounts, sign-ups, or personal information.
  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or location from this website.
  • We may show ads via Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to personalize those ads. You can opt out at adssettings.google.com.
  • Standard server logs (IP address, user agent, requested URL) are kept briefly for security and debugging. They are not sold or shared.

Information we don't collect

The dashboard at fires.nijemtech.com does not require you to identify yourself. There are no accounts, no logins, no forms, and no tracking pixels run by the site operator.

Information we do collect

Server logs

Like virtually any web server, our nginx instance logs each request with the visitor's IP address, the requested URL, the response code, and the user agent string. Logs rotate after 30 days and are used only for debugging and abuse detection.

Local browser storage

The site uses localStorage to remember your dismissal of the cookie-consent banner. That's it. No tracking, no analytics identifier.

Third-party advertising (Google AdSense)

This site participates in Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies and similar technologies to show ads based on your visits to this and other sites. Specifically:

  • Google uses the DoubleClick DART cookie to serve ads.
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting adssettings.google.com or by using the AdChoices icon shown on Google ads.
  • For more on how Google uses data from sites that use its services, see policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

Children

The site is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from them.

Your rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Because the site does not store personal data tied to you, there is generally nothing for us to access, export, or delete on your behalf. For data Google may have collected via AdSense cookies, please use Google's controls linked above.
